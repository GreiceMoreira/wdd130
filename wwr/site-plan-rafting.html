Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on)
The customers are mostly groups and families who prefer to spend time together. They range in age from their teens to their sixties.
Even if they are purchasing as a group, they expect to see package deals offered at the best prices.
What do they want?
What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by accessing the website?
They expect to find information such as our operating hours, the program schedule, and the cost per person or per group.
What are their needs that aren't being met?
How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?
They will likely access the site using a laptop to purchase tickets and use a mobile application while en route to the activity.
